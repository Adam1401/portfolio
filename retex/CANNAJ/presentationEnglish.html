  • Capgemini is one of the world leaders in consulting, IT services and outsourcing. It is a European ESN (Digital Service Company) in operation for 37 years, its head office is located in PARIS (75017) at the place of the Etoile and it also has two sites in Bordeaux.
    But the company is expanding globally, with a presence in 55 countries. It is found more particularly in Asia with China and Japan, then in Europe and finally in America with the United States and Canada. The legal form of the CapGemini company is a SAS, that is to say a simplified joint stock company, it is a company with shareholders who are taken into consideration (the profit is directly given to the shareholders according to their share), and who has a freely fixed capital (which can only vary by decision of the shareholders).
    The activities of the company are therefore as follows:
    • The design, development and integration of information systems.
    • Outsourcing services, i.e. the management of applications and business IT infrastructure.
    • Consulting services in the IT field. Capgemini's objective in the coming years is to be the leader in the transformation of industrial and technological companies as well as offers Cloud and data processing.
    As part of its CSR, this ESN has set up in collaboration with other companies of digital tools and participated in many actions:
    • Focus on the Code, #JNDJ, UnisCité
    • emmaüs connect, We Tech Care
    • OnDijon, Greener Mailbox
    • the digital fresco, INR
    While it becomes more and more complicated to assert oneself in the field of ESN, how does Capgemini manage to maintain its position as European leader?
